Recipe

Fried Tarantulas

Description

Creepy-crawly insides

Ingredients

  • Spider
  • Cornflakes
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Spray spider with oil
  2. Crush cornflakes
  3. Put spider in cornflakes (note, this will be easier if you are working with a dead spider)
  4. Drop in fryer filled with oil
  5. Take out when crisp
  6. Serve to guests and scar them for life
